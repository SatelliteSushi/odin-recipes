Litty Lasagna

Description

Indulge in layers of flavorful goodness with our Litty Lasagna recipe. This classic Italian dish features perfectly cooked pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and a generous topping of melted cheese. A comforting favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or pork
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown ground meat in a skillet; mix with marinara sauce
  3. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake until bubbly and golden; let it rest before serving.

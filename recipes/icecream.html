Nice Icecream

Description

Treat yourself to the simplicity of our Nice Ice Cream recipe. Creamy, smooth, and full of flavor, this homemade ice cream is a delightful dessert option. Choose your favorite mix-ins and savor the sweetness of a perfectly crafted frozen treat.

Ingredients:

  • Heavy cream
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Your choice of flavoring (e.g., fruit puree, chocolate chips)
  • Optional mix-ins (e.g., nuts, cookie crumbles)

Directions

  1. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Gently fold in sweetened condensed milk and vanilla.
  3. Mix in chosen flavoring and optional mix-ins.
  4. Transfer to a container; freeze until solid.
  5. Scoop and enjoy your homemade Nice Ice Cream!

Saucy Spaghetti

Description

Dive into a plate of Saucy Spaghetti that promises a burst of flavor in every bite. Our recipe features al dente spaghetti coated in a savory, aromatic tomato sauce. Garnished with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan, it's a pasta lover's dream come true.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Garlic and onions
  • Italian herbs (e.g., basil, oregano)

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti as directed; reserve a cup of pasta water.
  2. Brown meat in a pan; add garlic, onions, and herbs.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes and pasta water; simmer.
  4. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce.
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan.

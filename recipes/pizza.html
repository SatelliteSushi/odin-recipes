Pretty Pizza

Description

Elevate your pizza experience with our Pretty Pizza recipe. This visually appealing creation showcases a colorful medley of fresh, high-quality toppings on a crispy crust. Unleash your creativity and customize your pizza for a delicious and Instagram-worthy dining experience.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (e.g., bell peppers, olives, mushrooms, pepperoni)
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Directions

  1. Roll out pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  3. Add mozzarella and assorted toppings creatively.
  4. Bake until crust is golden and cheese melts.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil before slicing and serving.
